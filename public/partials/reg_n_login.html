Habitat For Humanity is based on a simple idea.  If good people join together to help give a "hand up" to someone in need, the world will move forward.  Habitat sells homes to good people in need of that "hand up".  Home buyers are able to use sweat equity hours as their down payment.  Habitat then sells them the home without charging a profit and provides the buyers with a no interest loan.  It works.
